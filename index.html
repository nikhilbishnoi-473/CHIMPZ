How To Buy

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $CHIMPZ. If you don't have any ETH, you can buy it directly on Metamask, transfer it from another wallet, or purchase it on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Connect to Uniswap. Go to app.uniswap.org in Google Chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $CHIMPZ token address into Uniswap, select CHIMPZ, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Download Metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or Google Play Store for free. Desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Switch ETH for $CHIMPZ. We do have taxes, and as of right now, they are set at 3%, so you will need to increase slippage to 4%. You may need to increase slippage even higher during times of market volatility.
